Financial Solutions That Grow With You

As a licensed microfinance institution regulated by UMRA, Grow Fund provides accessible financial services designed to empower individuals and small businesses across Northern Uganda. With branches in Lira, Gulu, and Kitgum, we're committed to your financial inclusion and success.

Our Core Services

Products designed for your financial growth

Salary Advance Loans

Short-term loans (1-18 months) for salaried individuals with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment terms.

  • Quick approval process
  • No collateral required
