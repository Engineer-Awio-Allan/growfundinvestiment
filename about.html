About Grow Fund Microfinance

Empowering Your Financial Future with Trust and Innovation

Background

Grow Fund Microfinance is committed to supporting civil servants, pensioners, and small business communities build a sustainable economic future through financial education and customized microfinance.

Established in April 2016, GrowFund Investment Limited is a registered and licensed microfinance entity fully regulated by the Uganda Microfinance Regulatory Authority (UMRA). Our headquarters are located at Muntu Investment Building, Te-Atat, Bar Ogole, Lira City West Division, Lira City.

Currently operating in northern Uganda with three branches (Lira City head office, Gulu City, and Kitgum Municipality), we have plans to extend our services throughout Uganda. Our team is highly valued for their expertise in market research and policy development.

We design and deliver financial packages that ensure our clients have access to competitive savings products, financial education, and promote social inclusion. Grow Fund provides financial advisory services and fast loans with terms up to eighteen months at affordable interest rates.

Our Purpose

To provide superior quality financial services by offering affordable loan products that suit clients' needs.
